Online food delivery

Zomato was founded by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, two Delhi IIT, in 2008. Till November 2010, Zomato was known as “Foodiebay.”

Zomato has made its feet strong and is now present in 22 countries with more than one million restaurants globally and receives 1.25 million orders daily.

Zomato received a total number of 909.6 million from different investors. Their recent funding was from Private Equity in 2020.

The main work of Zomato is to suggest local and nearby restaurants to users and receive orders from them. Users can place orders from their favorite restaurant based on ratings and reviews shared by previous customers.
